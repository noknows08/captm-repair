Services · 10 ways we can help

Every fix on the board.

Ten services, one workshop. Each one done by the same technician who answers your message — no ticket queue, no hand-offs, no jargon you didn't ask for.

SVC-01 · OS

PC Reformatting

Full wipe and clean OS reinstall — drivers, updates and essentials set up, bloat left behind. A genuinely fresh start.

SVC-02 · THERMAL

Deep Cleaning (Hardware)

Fans, heatsinks and boards stripped of dust and old paste. Better airflow, lower temps, no more overheating shutdowns.

SVC-03 · GPU

Game Installation (Triple-A Titles)

Big titles installed and configured properly — GPU drivers current, settings tuned to what your hardware can actually push.

SVC-04 · DISPLAY

LCD/Screen Replacement

Cracked, flickering or dead panels swapped on laptops and desktop monitors — hinge and cable checked while it's open.

SVC-05 · RAM/SSD

Hardware Upgrades

RAM, SSD/HDD, graphics cards and more — matched to your board and budget, fitted and tested before handover.

SVC-06 · SOFTWARE

Software Installation & Updates

Office tools, antivirus and the updates you keep postponing — installed, licensed correctly, and set to stay current.

SVC-07 · NETWORK

Network Setup

Routers configured, LAN cables run and terminated, dead zones and dropouts hunted down until the connection holds.

SVC-08 · DATA

Data Backup & Recovery

Files secured before any reformat, and lost data recovered where the drive still allows it. Your photos matter more than the OS.

SVC-09 · DIAG

Troubleshooting

Random crashes, blue screens, mystery slowdowns — diagnosed to the actual cause, not just rebooted and returned.

SVC-10 · I/O

Peripheral Support

Printers that won't print, scanners, webcams, mics and the rest — set up, driven correctly, and talking to your PC again.
